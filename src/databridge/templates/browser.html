Sampling Strategy

Enable to reduce export volume. Choose random, systematic, or stratified sampling and set the desired ratio.

Stratified Sampling

Records are bucketed by the Target Column value. The ratio or size quota is applied per group independently, so minority groups are preserved at the same rate as majority groups.

Ratio / Size Behaviour
0.0 – 1.0 Probability — each record kept with this chance (e.g. 0.1 ≈ 10 % of each group)
> 1 Absolute quota — keep at most this many records per group (e.g. 500 → up to 500 per stratum)

Note: the sampler is streaming — it does not know total group sizes in advance, so ratio mode is probabilistic.
